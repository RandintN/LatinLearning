The booklets below are extracted from the files of the Thesaurus Precum Latinarum. They are .pdf files ready for printing. To view and print the .pdf files, you will need Adobe Acrobat Reader 5.0 (or later), or some other compatible program that can read .pdf files. Each booklet is designed to be printed double-sided (i.e. front and back sides of each sheet of paper) and then folded in half to form a booklet. If your printer does not support double-sided printing directly, then print the odd pages first, turn the paper over, feed it back into your printer, and then print the even pages (Adobe Acrobat Reader 5.0 supports odd/even page printing). If you have never done this before, you might want to print a few pages as a test first to make sure you have the procedure right first. Once all the pages have been printed, fold them in half to form the booklet.

The paper size is assumed to be 8.5in x 11in, and thus the booklet will be half that, or 8.5in x 5.5in. Since pdf's can be easily scaled, you can scale the printing to other paper sizes as desired.
