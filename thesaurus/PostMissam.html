Prayers and devotions for after Mass and the recpetion of Communion.
Ex Missali Romano (From the Roman Missal)


The following prayers are taken from the 1962 Roman Missal. They had been added to the Roman Missal after the Council of Trent (1545-1563). Many of them had been used in the Church for private devotion well before the Council.
  • Oratio Post Missam
         Prayer After Mass
  • Oratio Sancti Thomae Aquinatis Post Missam
         Prayer of St. Thomas Aquinas After Mass
  • Oratio Sancti Bonaventurae
         Prayer of Saint Bonaventure
  • Anima Christi
         Soul of Christ
  • Oblatio Sui
         Prayer of Self-Dedication to Jesus Christ
  • En ego, o bone et dulcissime Iesu
         Prayer Before a Crucifix
  • Oratio Universalis
         Universal Prayer
  • Obsecro te, dulcissime Domine Iesu Christe
         I Beseech Thee, Most Sweet Lord Jesus Christ
  • Oratio ad Mariam Virginem Post Missam
         Prayer to the Blessed Virgin Mary After Mass
  • Oratio ad Sanctum Ioseph
         Prayer to St. Joseph
  • Oratio ad Sanctum Post Missam
         Prayer After Mass to the Day's Saint
