Deliver me, Lord Jesus Christ		 Confessio


From the Roman Missal.

LIBERA me, Domine, Iesu Christe, ab omnibus iniquitatibus meis et universis malis, fac me tuis semper inhaerere mandatis et a te numquam separari permittas. Amen. DELIVER me, Lord Jesus Christ, from all my iniquities and from every evil, make me hold ever fast to Thy commandments and never allow me to be separated from Thee. Amen.
Raccolta #109. (S. P. Ap., Nov. 12, 1938).
