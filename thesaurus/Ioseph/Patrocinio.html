Contents Patrocinio Ioseph Suffulti
Supported by the Patronage of Joseph		 Ioseph


This prayer is the Secret for the votive Mass for St. Joseph from the '62 Roman Missal.

SANCTISSIMAE Genetricis tuae Sponsi patrocinio suffulti, rogamus, Domine, clementiam tuam: ut corda nostra facias terrena cuncta despicere ac te verum Deum perfectum caritate diligere: Qui vivis et regnas in saecula saeculorum. Amen SUPPORTED by the patronage of the Spouse of Thy most holy Mother, we pray Thee, O Lord, for Thy mercy; that Thou wouldst make our hearts despise all things earthly and love Thee, the true God, with perfect charity: Thou Who livest and reignest world without end. Amen.
