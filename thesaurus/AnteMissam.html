Prayers and devotions for before Mass and recpetion of Communion.
Ex Missali Romano (From the Roman Missal)


The following prayers are taken from the 1962 Roman Missal. They had been added to the Roman Missal after the Council of Trent (1545-1563). Many of them had been used in the Church for private devotion well before the Council.
  • Oratio Ante Missam
         Prayer Before Mass
  • Orationes Sancti Ambrosii Ante Missam singulis hebdomadae diebus distributae
         Prayers of Saint Ambrose before Mass for each day of the week
  • Oratio Sancti Ambrosii Ante Missam
         Prayer of St. Ambrose Before Mass
  • Oratio Sancti Thomae Aquinatis Ante Missam
         Prayer of St. Thomas Aquinas Before Mass
  • Oratio ad Beatam Mariam Virginem Ante Missam
         Prayer to the Blessed Virgin Mary Before Mass
  • Preces Ad Sanctum Ioseph Ante Missam
         Prayer to St. Joseph Before Mass
  • Oratio Ad Omnes Angelos et Sanctos Ante Missam
         Prayer to All the Angels and Saints Before Mass
  • Oratio ad Sanctum in cuius Honorem Missa Celebratur
         Prayer to the Saint in Whose Honor the Mass is Celebrated
  • Formula Intensionis Ante Missam
         Statement of Intention
