O Good Jesu		 Filius
O BONE Iesu, secundum magnam misericordiam tuam miserere mei. O clementissime Iesu, te deprecor per illum Sanguinem pretiosum, quem pro peccatoribus effundere voluisti, ut abluas omnes iniquitates meas et in me respicias miserum et indignum invocantem tuum sanctum Nomen. Ergo, Iesu, propter Nomen sanctum tuum salva me. Amen. O GOOD Jesu, according to Thy great mercy, have mercy on me. O most merciful Jesu, by that Precious Blood which Thou didst will to shed for sinners, I beseech Thee to wash away all mine iniquities and to look graciously upon me, a poor and unworthy sinner, as I call upon Thy holy Name. Therefore, O Jesus, do Thou save me for Thy holy Name's sake. Amen.
From the Raccolta #121. (S. C. Ind., Nov. 26, 1896; S. P. Ap., Dec. 17, 1932).
