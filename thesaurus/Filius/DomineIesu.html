Contents Domine Iesu
Lord, Jesus		 Filius
DOMINE Iesu, LORD Jesus,
PER vagitus Tui in praesepio pro me nascentis, BY Thine infant cries when Thou wast born for me in the manger,
PER lacrimas Tui in cruce pro me morientis, BY Thy tears when Thou didst die for me on the Cross,
PER amorem Tui in tabernaculo pro me viventis, BY Thy love as Thou livest for me in the tabernacle,
miserere mei et salva me. Have mercy on me and save me.
From the Raccolta #93 (S. P. Ap., Jan. 20 1941)
