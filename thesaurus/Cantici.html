A canticle is a lyric or song of thanksgiving that is not from the Psalms, but composed by a famous person of the Old or New Testament. There are a total of 38 canticles used in the Liturgy of the Hours. Below are the three common ones used in the Liturgy of the Hours.
  • Benedictus
         Canticle of Zechariah
  • Magnificat
         Canticle of Mary
  • Nunc Dimittis
         Canticle of Simeon
