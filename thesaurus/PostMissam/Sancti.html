Prayer After Mass to the Day's Saint		 PostMissam


From the Roman Missal.

SANCTE(A) N., in cuius honorem incruentum Corporis et Sanguinis Christi sacrificium obtuli, fac, tua potenti apud Deum intercessione, ut, usu huius mysterii, passionis et mortis eiusdem Christi Salvatoris nostri merita consequar, ac, cum illius frequentatione, continuo crescat meae salutis effectus. Amen. O SAINT N., in whose honor I have offered the bloodless sacrifice of the Body and Blood of Christ, grant by thy powerful intercession with God, that, by the utility of this mystery, I may obtain the merits of the passion and death of this same Christ our Savior. And with its frequency come to salvation. Amen.
