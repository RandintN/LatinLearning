Contents Pro defuncta nominatim
For a Deceased Woman		 Varia


From the Roman Missal.

QUAESUMUS, Domine, pro tua pietate miserere animae famulae tuae N., et a contagiis mortalitatis exutam, in aeternae salvationis partem restitue. Per Christum Dominum nostrum. Amen. WE beseech Thee, O Lord, according to Thy loving-kindness, have mercy upon the soul of Thy handmaiden N., and now that she is set free from the defilements of this mortal flesh, restore her to her heritage of everlasting salvation. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.
From the Raccolta #600 (S. P. Ap., March 14, 1923 and June 24, 1936)
