Numbers to Remember		 Home


The list below was compiled from a number of prayer books that were composed from the 18th to the mid 20th century. Prayer books from this period would often contain short summaries on the teachings of the Church and the duties of good Christians. The sources for the texts of each of the items below are the Catechismus Catholicae Ecclesiae, 1997 (CCC) and the Catechismus Catholicus, Petri Cardinalis Gaspari, 1931. Lest anyone think that the Church today feels that some of the items below are no longer relevant in this modern age, the applicable section numbers from the CCC have been given where appropriate.
  • Praecepta Decalogi
         The Ten Commandments
  • Sex Praecepta Ecclesiae
         The Six Precepts of the Church
  • Duodecim Apostoli
         The Twelve Apostles
  • Septem Sacramenta
         The Seven Sacraments
  • Tres Virtutes Theologales
         The Three Theological Virtues
  • Quattuor Virtutes Cardinales
         The Four Cardinal Virtues
  • Septem Dona Spiritus Sancti
         The Seven Gifts of the Holy Spirit
  • Duodecim Fructus Spiritus Sancti
         The Twelve Fruits of the Holy Spirit
  • Septem Opera Misericordiae Spiritualia
         The Seven Spiritual Works of Mercy
  • Septem Opera Misericordiae Corporalia
         The Seven Corporal Works of Mercy
  • Octo Beatitudines
         The Eight Beatitudes
  • Septem peccata capitalia et virtutes opponuntur
         The Seven Deadly Sins and their opposite virtues
  • Sex Peccata in Spiritum Sanctum
         The Six Sins against the Holy Spirit
  • Quattuor Peccata Quae ad Caelum Clamant
         The Four Sins Crying to Heaven for Vengeance
  • Novem viae conscius in peccato alii esse
         Nine Ways of being an Accessory to another's Sin.
  • Tria Praecipua bonorum operum genera
         Three Eminent Good Works
  • Consilia Evangelica
         The Evangelical Counsels
  • De Quattuor Novissimis Memorandis
         The Four Last Things to be Remembered
  • Subjects for Daily Meditation.
