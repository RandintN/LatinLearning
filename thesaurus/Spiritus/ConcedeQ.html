Contents Concede, quaesumus
Grant, we beseech Thee		 Spiritus


From the Roman Missal

CONCEDE, quaesumus, omnipotens Deus, Sanctum nos Spiritum votis promereri sedulis, quatenus eius gratia et ab omnibus liberemur tentationibus, et peccatorum nostrorum indulgentiam percipere mereamur. Per Christum Dominum nostrum. Amen. GRANT, we beseech Thee, Almighty God, that we may so please Thy Holy Spirit by our earnest entreaties, that we may by His grace both be freed from all temptations and merit to receive the forgiveness of our sins. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.
From the Raccolta #59; (S. P. Ap., Nov. 22, 1934)
